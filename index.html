Akshit Kumar

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Center for Algorithms, Data, and Market Design at Yale University. I recently graduated from the Decision, Risk and Operations division at Columbia Business School, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Omar Besbes and Prof. Yash Kanoria. Prior to Columbia, I completed my Master's in Electrical Engineering from the University of Michigan, and before that I graduated with a B.Tech (Hons.) in Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Madras. I am broadly interested in the operations of online platforms and marketplaces, with a particular focus on AI agents and recommendation systems.
