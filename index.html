Akshit Kumar

I am rising third PhD student at Columbia Business School , where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Yash Kanoria and Prof. Omar Besbes . My current research interests are network revenue management, dynamic matching and reinforcement learning theory.

Previously, I completed my Masters in Electrical Engineering from University of Michigan where I am worked with Prof. Vijay Subramanian on theoretical reinforcement learning and stochastic control. I have been fortunate to work with an amazing set of advisors in the past. I completed my bachelors thesis with Prof. Rahul Vaze at Tata Institute of Fundamental Research . I also interned at Microsoft Research India, where I was advised by Dr. Ranveer Chandra and Dr. Manohar Swaminathan .

I graduated with a B.Tech (Hons.) in Electrical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology, Madras .
